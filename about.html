About Pharaoh Motors

Founded in the heart of Alexandria in 1960 by the visionary engineer Ahmed Ragab, Car Factory was born from a simple yet powerful dream: to build a car for every Egyptian family. In an era of industrial growth, Ragab saw an opportunity to create a vehicle that was not only robust and reliable enough for our unique roads but also affordable and proud in its design.

The first "Nile Eagle" model rolled off our assembly line just two years later, marking the beginning of a long journey in automotive excellence and establishing our commitment to quality and national pride.
